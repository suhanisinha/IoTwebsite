What is this About?

This page is all about managing and controlling your

Iot Development Board from a website by a simple click

on the the component you are currently using.

Simply connect the component you wish to operate

by the jumper wires and connect to see how you can

remotely manage your board. Isn't it fascinating?

Try one yourself!

Light Emitting Diode

Connect a LED to your NODE MCU and click on the buttons given below to turn it on/off.

TURN ON LED TURN OFF LED

7 Segment

Connect a 7 Segment display to your NODE MCU and click on the buttons given below to display any number.

DISPLAY 1 DISPLAY 2 DISPLAY 3

DISPLAY 4 DISPLAY 5 DISPLAY 6

DISPLAY 7 DISPLAY 8 DISPLAY 9

DISPLAY 0

BUZZER

Connect a Buzzer to your NODE MCU and click on the buttons given below to turn it on/off.

TURN ON BUZZER TURN OFF BUZZER

DC MOTOR

Connect a DC Motor to your NODE MCU and click on the buttons given below to turn it on/off.

TURN ON MOTOR TURN OFF MOTOR
